Spiced Sweet Potato Salad

Description

This salad is super easy to make and always a hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 0.5 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • chopped dates
  • chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • spring onions
  • 2 tbsp date syrup
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • juice of half a lemon

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C
  2. Add the spices and olive oil to the sweet potato cubes and mix well, ensuring everything gets coated evenly. Cook for 40-45 minutes, until soft.
  3. While the sweet potatoes are cooking, make the dressing by whisking all of the ingredients together until smooth.
  4. Once the sweet potatoes are cooked, mix with the dressing, parsley and peanuts and top with sesame seeds and spring onions.
