Banana Bread

Description

This is the best Banana Bread recipe I've ever made! Super easy and quick, I recommend!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • chocolate chips
  • handful of walnuts

Steps

  1. Mix mashed bananas, vanilla and melted coconut oil together in a bowl
  2. In a separate bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and baking soda
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix to combine
  4. Pour into a parchment paper-lined loaf pan and bake at 350 F for 60 minutes or until browned and cracked on top
  5. Enjoy!
